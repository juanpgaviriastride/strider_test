Webtalk Products and Services

Built In Networks & Groups

Webtalk has filed patents on the way people connect within Webtalk. First, using built in networks, you can choose to add someone as a Professional Connection, Personal Connection or both. Then you can only select one built in group per network for each new connection. Professional connections are offered one subgroup as well. Those options are Current, Former or Prospect, which will help you channel your sales and recruiting communication better.

Contacts Management

Webtalk uses the built in network, group and subgroup feature as a way to easily filter and access the connections you have made. Think of it as a virtual Roledex. You can write a description and keywords to help you both search for your connection at a later date and read your notes on why you made the connections.

Profile

Webtalk’s profile is unique by offering all users the ability to fully customize their banners and write full width taglines across the top. The profile also allows all users to showcase the files, photos and videos they want to share with the public and individual networks creating a more engaging experience. Webtalk offers a virtual resume type of profile as the default information and based on the group you have been added to will determine each user’s access level to another user’s media and content.

Network News

Webtalk has filed two patents on our real time network news feed. The first is the ability to post and display news by our built in networks, groups and subgroups and even the pages each user follows. The second patent is on a keyword and media attachment search feature. Webtalk users are able to search the entire history of their network news feed by keyword and then sort them based on the type of media (files/photos/videos) attached to them.

Files

Webtalk uses the built in network, group and subgroup feature as a way to easily filter and access the files you have uploaded and shared on Webtalk. All media when loaded to Webtalk is private by default giving each user a secure virtual desktop until they decide to share it. As of today, files loaded can only be viewed, but coming soon each user will be able to edit and collaborate on documents.

Photos

Webtalk uses the built in network, group and subgroup feature as a way to easily filter and access the photos you have uploaded and shared on Webtalk. All media when loaded to Webtalk is private by default giving each user a secure virtual desktop until they decide to share it. As of today, photos loaded can only be viewed, edited and tagged, but coming soon Webtalk will be offering a “Pin” feature where each user can pin a comment to a certain point on an image for realtime collaboration.

Videos

Webtalk uses the built in network, group and subgroup feature as a way to easily filter and access the videos you have uploaded and shared on Webtalk. All media when loaded to Webtalk is private by default giving each user a secure virtual desktop until they decide to share it. As of today, videos can only be stored and shared by posting or saving a YouTube or Vimeo link.

Real-Time Chat

Webtalk’s real-time chat sends push notifications and email notifications as soon as the message is sent. It also shows when another person is typing and who is online. Chat is free for communicating between connections, but requires a Pro upgrade when attempting to chat with non-connections.

Search

Webtalk’s search allow you to search for people, companies, brands, job titles, industry and more using advanced search filters. All advanced search filters, such as the ability to find people looking for jobs or search by age range or gender, require a PRO upgrade.

Pages for Businesses & Celebrities

Pages for businesses, brands and celebrities are similarly unique like profiles with a couple distinct differences. When you create a page, you are given three addon tab options to choose from including Products, Services or Portfolio. In the current release, you can sell your products and services by linking your website to each item posted. In future releases, you will be able to sell products and services directly on Webtalk. Pages are also offered private connections with staff, which gives each page owner a private enterprise social network for their business.

Webtalk Pro

Webtalk offers exclusive features through a premium upgrade service called Pro. These features include, but are not limited to, sending messages to non-connections, see who has viewed your profile, advanced search filters and large file storage (over 50MBs in size). This service starts at $15 per month.

iMeet by Webtalk

Webtalk has partnered up with PGI to white label and resell a virtual meeting service called iMeet. Webtalk users are given discounted prices to purchase iMeet through Webtalk instead of on iMeet.com iMeet and iMeetLive products allow Webtalk users to host virtual conferences and webcasts for up to 10,000 people with prices starting at only $22 per month. All iMeet packages include video conferencing, screen sharing, recording, call me dialing, analytics and mobile app conferencing.

SocialCPX: Being social pays BIG!

Instead of investing tens of millions of dollars into online marketing, Webtalk invested in a unique affiliate program software to drive exponential growth. The CPX stands for Commission Per Exchange, or commission based marketing. Webtalk has filed patents on both the commission plan for the affiliate program and also the social tools integrated to make the commission plan work. Through SocialCPX, Webtalk shares up to 50% of all revenue and is able to offer affiliates residual lifetime commissions on all subscription and advertising revenue generated by the users they drive to Webtalk.

SocialCPX Dashboard

This dashboard tracks all of the links embedded into media, emails, text messages, banner ads and buttons shared by affiliates to give each affiliate an accurate overview of their progress. It tracks the number of clicks, signups, revenue generated and commissions. It also has a banking platform where their commissions will be deposited and can be transferred to their personal bank accounts, PayPal, Webtalk Credits or to their Webtalk MasterCard Payroll card. There are also a number of built in training tools to help all affiliates become successful.

SocialCPX for Advertisers

On SocialCPX, advertisers can apply to run an advertising campaign on Webtalk. The largest incentives to advertise on Webtalk is the CPX campaign platform, which offers no upfront cost advertising that includes detailed analytics about our advertiser’s customers for free. CPX campaigns are all commission based. Advertisers only pay Webtalk when we make a sale. Webtalk also offers traditional pay per click (CPC) and impression (CPM) advertising options.

Webtalk PrintShop

Webtalk partnered with VistaPrint to offer a wide variety of Webtalk and SocialCPX branded clothing and promotional items. The PrintShop also sells fully customized products for affiliates that contain their affiliate links and QR codes, including but not limited to; affiliate business cards, t-shirts, pens, hats, cups, bags and mousepads. VistaPrint guarantees the quality of every product and every customer is backed by both Webtalk and VistaPrint support.

Webtalk HelpDesk

Our help desk offers detailed answers for just about any question you can think of regarding Webtalk’s products and services. The help desk is broken down to four departments; user support, customer support, affiliate support and advertiser support. If you can’t find the answer to your question, you can send an email to Webtalk’s support staff and they will reply within three business days. If you need urgent support, you can upgrade to premium support, which includes live chat and call in support for only $20 per month.

Team Trainer

Any Webtalk user who enrolls in Webtalk’s free SocialCPX affiliate referral program is eligible to become a Team Trainer and earn commissions from indirect referrals (referrals of your referrals) through five referral levels. All affiliates will instantly be awarded the Team Trainer promotion once one of the affiliate’s direct referrals has purchased a Webtalk premium service; including but not limited to; iMeet virtual meetings, Webtalk Pro exclusive feature subscription and Premium phone and live chat support. Webtalk Credit purchases DO NOT QUALIFY for the Team Trainer promotion UNTIL they are applied towards the purchase of a Webtalk premium service. The one premium service sales quota requirement is an ongoing requirement through the entire enrollment period as a SocialCPX affiliate. Annual premium service purchases award Team Trainer qualifications for a year. However, a single monthly service purchase must renew monthly to maintain Team Trainer qualifications. Affiliates must maintain one (1) active premium service direct referral customer to maintain Team Trainer qualifications.

Affiliate Commission Plan

All users who have enrolled as an affiliate will immediately begin earning a TEN PERCENT (10%) commission on all revenue generated from their direct referrals. Once an affiliate has been awarded the Team Trainer designation, they are eligible to earn the same TEN PERCENT (10%) commission from all revenue generated by a five (5) level user referral network, which includes four (4) levels of indirect referrals plus direct referrals. Commissions paid on revenue share includes, but is not limited to; advertising sales, promotional offer purchases, premium service subscription, talent solutions, transactions fees and third party software application subscription and download fees. Commissions are paid on Webtalk’s gross revenue, not on gross sales, and therefore purchases made from third party providers will hold a much lower gross revenue total than the gross sales total (ie. Webtalk makes no guarantees on earnings.
