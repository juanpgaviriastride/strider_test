Webtalk Prelaunch Terms & Conditions

Crowdfunding

Webtalk’s Pre-launch is a *risk-free crowdfunding campaign built to acquire pre-sales of Webtalk’s Credits that will be valid towards the purchase of any premium service offering upon launch. The sales revenue acquired will be used to show traction to venture capitalist in an effort to assist in raising the funds Webtalk needs to sustain operations after launch. Half of all revenue will be reserved in a segregated account for future commission awards and the other half will be applied to the operating expenses of bringing Webtalk’s products to market.

Webtalk Credits

Credit purchases are not refundable and must be applied to any of the following services after Webtalk’s launch; iMeet virtual meeting service, Webtalk Pro exclusive feature subscriptions and/or premium phone and live chat support. Credits can also be applied towards the purchase of advertising on Webtalk upon launch. Credits do not expire and therefore can be held and applied to any future product offerings by Webtalk. No affiliate commissions are paid on Webtalk Credit purchases until they are applied to a Webtalk premium service as described below in the Team Trainer section.

Risk-Free

In the event that Webtalk is unable to launch its full product offering by the announced deadline on Webtalk.co, the company will apply any Webtalk Credits purchased towards the purchase of an iMeet Plus virtual meeting service for three months. For more details about the iMeet Plus service plan, please visit http://iMeet.com. Webtalk is an official reseller for PGi’s iMeet virtual meeting services. Webtalk reserves the right to extend the announced deadline to launch all products until December 31, 2016. If the deadline is not fulfilled by the extended date, all Webtalk Credits purchased will immediately be applied to three months of iMeet Plus service which shall be activated, and will have received activation notice, by no later than January 31st, 2017.

Referral Rewards

In order to receive commissions, all Webtalk prelaunch users must enroll in Webtalk’s free SocialCPX affiliate referral program upon the full product launch. Once you have enrolled, all commissions from your direct referrals to Webtalk’s Pre-launch will be available immediately. All indirect referrals (people referred by your referrals) through five referral levels fall under the Team Trainer terms and conditions of the affiliate referral program.

Team Trainer

Any Webtalk user who enrolls in Webtalk’s free SocialCPX affiliate referral program is eligible to become a Team Trainer and earn commissions from indirect referrals (referrals of your referrals) through five referral levels. All affiliates will instantly be awarded the Team Trainer promotion once one of the affiliate’s direct referrals has purchased a Webtalk premium service; including but not limited to; iMeet virtual meetings, Webtalk Pro exclusive feature subscription and Premium phone and live chat support. Webtalk Credit purchases DO NOT QUALIFY for the Team Trainer promotion UNTIL they are applied towards the purchase of a Webtalk premium service. The one premium service sales quota requirement is an ongoing requirement through the entire enrollment period as a SocialCPX affiliate. Annual premium service purchases award Team Trainer qualifications for a year. However, a single monthly service purchase must renew monthly to maintain Team Trainer qualifications. Affiliates must maintain one (1) active premium service direct referral customer to maintain Team Trainer qualifications.

Guidelines & Restrictions

Direct referrals to qualify Team Trainer eligibility cannot be anyone residing within the affiliate’s household. Affiliates will be immediately terminated from the SocialCPX affiliate program if any the following conditions apply; an affiliate makes a purchase under another Webtalk user’s account to qualify for the Team Trainer promotion, an affiliate misrepresents the earning potential of Webtalk affiliate program, an affiliate misrepresents or defames any company brand, likeness or employee of Webtalk, Inc., or an affiliate violates any of Webtalk’s Terms, Conditions, Copyrights, Trademarks or Patents. All users agree that they will not attempt to de-engineer, reverse-engineer or attempt to gain unauthorized access to any of Webtalk’s internet products.

Affiliate Commission Plan

All users who have enrolled as an affiliate will immediately begin earning a TEN PERCENT (10%) commission on all revenue generated from their direct referrals. Once an affiliate has been awarded the Team Trainer designation, they are eligible to earn the same TEN PERCENT (10%) commission from all revenue generated by a five (5) level user referral network, which includes four (4) levels of indirect referrals plus direct referrals. Commissions paid on revenue share includes, but is not limited to; advertising sales, promotional offer purchases, premium service subscription, talent solutions, transactions fees and third party software application subscription and download fees. Commissions are paid on Webtalk’s gross revenue, not on gross sales, and therefore purchases made from third party providers will hold a much lower gross revenue total than the gross sales total (ie. Best Buy sells a game system for $200 on Webtalk and pays Webtalk $20 for the sale, only the $20 is commissionable to affiliates). Individual results will vary. Webtalk makes no guarantees on earnings.
